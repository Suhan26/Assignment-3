Professional Program

The PSA Hockey Skating program is designed to teach skating instructors the proper methods of teaching hockey skating. The primary focus is up-to-date techniques to help hockey players become better skaters. Courses are progressive with one building upon the other. There are currently four levels of certification from Hockey Skating 1 to Hockey Skating 4. Hockey Skating 1, 2, and 3 are currently offered in the e-learning platform. Hockey Skating 4 may be offered at the PSA Summit or a hockey-specific seminar or clinic.
